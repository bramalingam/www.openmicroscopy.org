Explore OME in Action

OME products are in use in labs and research institutes around the world. Explore what they can do for you with this selection of demos and live online tools.

OME for Scientists

Whether you have your own microscopy images or want to test functionality with publicly available data, there's a tool or demo for you to try

Sign up for our OMERO demo server to test out the functionality with your own data.

Already using ImageJ/Fiji? Check out the Bio-Formats plugin.

Test out OMERO.web functionality with the Image Data Resource, making datasets from published scientific studies publicly available for viewing and data reuse and reanalysis.

Try the interactive OMERO.figure demo to see the power of using OMERO to present your data.

OME at Institutions

Explore examples of public OMERO.servers

The LINCS program at Harvard Medical School provides a great example of how an OMERO.server can be configured to host public images, allowing individual scientists to link to their own images for sharing.

The Queensland Brain Institute's Advanced Microimaging and Analysis Facility also uses OMERO, including to host public images.
